Down-step in dimension dim for \((\phi_i(x),\phi_j(x))_{L_2}\).

Applies the down-part of the one-dimensional mass matrix in one dimension. Computes

\[\int_{x=0}^1 \phi_i(x) \sum_{j, l_i\geq l_j} \alpha_j \phi_j(x) dx.\]

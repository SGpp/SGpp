Detailed Description

The ElasticNetFunction class.

Corresponds to the regularization functional \( \left(1 - \gamma \right) \Vert \boldsymbol{\alpha} \Vert_2 + \gamma \Vert \boldsymbol{\alpha} \Vert _1 \).
